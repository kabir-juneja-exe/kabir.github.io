Ticket System

A simple yet powerful Container-based ticket system for Discord servers.
Requires variables: ticket (value= 0), embedid (value= none), and belongsto (value= none)

BDScript 2 
$nomention
$deletecommand
$addContainer[ticket]
$addSeparator[true;;ticket]
$addTextDisplay[**BASIC**
> General Inquiries
> Questions About This Server;ticket]
$addSeparator[true;;ticket]
$addTextDisplay[**ADVANCED**
> Report Staff or Member
> Partnership Request;ticket]
$addSeparator[true;;ticket]
$addActionRow[ticketbutton;ticket]
$addButtonCV2[general;Basic Support;secondary;no;;ticketbutton]
$addButtonCV2[advanced;Advanced Support;secondary;no;;ticketbutton]
$addTextDisplay[-# This can be used as your footer;ticket]
$addMediaGallery[media1;ticket]
$addMediaGalleryItem[Media URL;;;media1]
